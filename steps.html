Steps

In previous sections, we worked with animals’ names and adjectives both comparative and superlative. This is an amazing topic because everyone likes animals and learning about them is very curious. In today's lesson, we're taking a look at the animal world and finding information about peculiar animals. Let’s explore!

1. LESSON 1 - Real or Fantastic?

Lesson 1. There are magical creatures in literature and cinema that can be inspiring, but in real life there are creatures that are just as amazing. In order to develop this project, your partner and you must follow these instructions:

a) find an amazing animal online.

b) describe the characteristics this animal: physical description, habitat, food, the reason why it is peculiar... To do it, you must complete the fact file on your computer. Find it below.

c) brief presentation about your animal in the second lesson.

2. A world full of surprising animals

You already know what your challenge is and the activity that will bring you closer to the goal. But to succeed in your journey, you will need some strategies that will help you in this and other similar tasks. Before you think or look for your animal online, pay attention to the characteristics of the animal. They must be special for a reason, find out facts about where they live, what they eat...  For example:

The tiger is an amazing animal. It lives in the South East of Asia and it's a carnivore animal...

At this point, you have chosen your favorite animal!

 

3. Getting ideas!

Each animal is special in a different way. What makes these animals special?  Think of your favourite animal, what makes it better than the other animals? Look at the following images and compare two animals. Don't forget to use comparative and superlative adjectives to describe animals. For example:

The cat is smaller than the lion. // The lion is the wildest animal.
